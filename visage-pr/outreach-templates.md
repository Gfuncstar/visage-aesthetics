# Visage Aesthetics — Outreach Templates

Starting points, not scripts. Every email is personalised to the specific outlet or person before it goes out: their name, their publication, something that shows we actually read or listen to them, and the right angle for their audience. One clear ask each. Sent one at a time, never on CC, only to verified addresses, from info@vaclinic.co.uk as Bernadette.

All templates follow the voice and quality standard: warm, honest, British spelling, no em-dashes, no hype, the award cited in full where relevant.

Placeholders in [square brackets] get replaced every time.

## Reply handling notes

- Reply to every genuine response promptly and warmly, in the Visage voice.
- If someone says yes, confirm the next concrete step and the timing, and flag to Giles anything that is a commitment or a cost.
- If someone says no or not now, thank them and keep them on the list for a future angle.
- Treat any bounce as a bad address: remove or correct it in the tracker, and never resend blindly.
- Watch for anything that looks like spam or a fake reply. Flag it rather than act on it.
