## Read this first (honesty notes)

- Research was done by web search. The official pages were not all openable directly in this environment, so treat every fee and date below as "confirm with one click on the official page before acting". Each row has the official URL.
- Today is 2026-05-20. Several 2026 award cycles have already closed or happened. Those are marked.
- We only list awards a consumer clinic can enter. Practitioner-training and education awards are deliberately left out, to keep Visage separate from the sister education brand.
- Awards can be national, not just local. A national clinic award is a credibility asset we can use in local promotion. The only filter, given no budget, is that the award must be free to enter.
- Visage already holds Best Non-Surgical Aesthetics Clinic 2026, Essex (Health, Beauty & Wellness Awards). That is the headline asset to build PR around now, separate from entering anything new.

## Recommendation (no budget, free and earned only)

There is no budget. So we only chase awards that are genuinely free, and we get the value from the existing award and from free entries, not from paying for trophies. The rule of thumb: an award is only worth our time if entering costs nothing and the exposure is free.

Run these, in order:

1. **Squeeze the award we already hold (free, do this first).** Best Non-Surgical Aesthetics Clinic 2026, Essex is already won and paid for by no one. It is the headline of the first wave of local press, the new press page, Google Business Profile posts, and social. This is the highest-return, zero-cost move. No new entry needed.
2. **Muddy Stilettos Essex (free start to finish).** The best free local consumer award. It is won on turnout, not on a paid entry, which suits us perfectly: we already have happy patients, Google reviewers and an Instagram following to mobilise. The clever play is to line up that voting push now so we are ready the moment nominations open. The 2026 window has most likely closed, so this is the 2027 target. Confirm the dates on the official page. Campaign kit is in [awards/muddy-stilettos-essex-campaign.md](awards/muddy-stilettos-essex-campaign.md).
3. **LUXlife re-nomination (free).** The Health, Beauty & Wellness Awards run an open-vote cycle each year with no obligatory cost. We can be re-nominated for free and try to retain or upgrade the title. Low effort, keep it ticking over.
4. **Free-to-enter business awards, for the finalist exposure only (free, but read the catch).** Business Awards UK (Essex and Health & Wellbeing) and the English Hair & Beauty Awards are reportedly free to enter. The clever move is to enter for the free "finalist" badge and the PR mention, not for a trophy. Watch the catch: some of these charge an acceptance or ceremony fee if you win, and that is a spend we will not make without Giles's say-so. Confirm on the official page first. A reusable written entry is ready in [awards/free-entry-application.md](awards/free-entry-application.md).

**Not pursuing on no budget:** The Aesthetics Awards (around £190 per entry), MyFaceMyBody (paid), Safety in Beauty (invitation and membership), and any paid magazine award package. Park these unless a budget ever appears.

Always confirm the fee and deadline on the official page before entering anything. If any award turns out to cost money to enter or to accept, it stops and goes to Giles first.
