# Visage Aesthetics — Local Media List

Real local outlets for pitching consumer stories that drive clinic bookings. Compiled 2026-05-20 from web research.

## Read this first (honesty and rules)

- **Confirm before sending.** These contacts were gathered by web search. The live contact pages could not all be opened directly in this environment, so every email below needs a final check on the outlet's own contact page before we send. Our rule stands: verified addresses only, never send blind. The Status column shows relative confidence, not page-level proof.
- **No budget, earned only.** We pitch for free editorial coverage. We do not buy advertising, advertorial or paid features. Some outlets below are commercial (they sell features). Those are marked. With no budget we approach them only for genuine editorial, and we walk away from anything that asks for payment.
- **Local catchment, around 20 miles of Braintree (CM7 9BL).** The audience we care about is Braintree, Witham, Halstead, Great Dunmow, Coggeshall, Kelvedon, Chelmsford, Colchester, Maldon, Sudbury, Saffron Walden, Bishop's Stortford and the villages between. County and regional titles that cover these towns are in scope. A purely national consumer outlet is not.
- **Stale names change.** Several editors had moved on at the time of research. Always get the current name before pitching.

## Priority targets (start here)

1. **Braintree & Witham Times** — the single most relevant local paper. Lead with the award and the nurse-led safety angle.
2. **Actual Radio (Colchester)** — independent, explicitly covers Braintree and Witham, receptive to community stories.
3. **Muddy Stilettos Essex** — ideal audience, but commercial. Pitch for genuine editorial only, and tie in with the free awards campaign.
4. **Essex Magazine** — named editors with on-domain emails.
5. **BBC Essex** — county radio, good for the safety and consumer-protection angle.
