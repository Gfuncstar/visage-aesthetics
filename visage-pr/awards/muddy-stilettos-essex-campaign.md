# Muddy Stilettos Essex Awards — Free Campaign Kit

Muddy Stilettos Essex is free start to finish and is a perfect local consumer fit. It is not won on a clever written entry. It is won on turnout: nominations, then a public vote. That suits us, because we already have happy patients, Google reviewers and an Instagram following. The clever play is to be ready and to mobilise them quickly when the window opens.

Confirm the current dates on the official page before acting: https://essex.muddystilettos.co.uk/awards/

Typical cycle (from research, confirm each year): nominations in March, regional voting April to May, winners in June. If this year's window has closed, prepare now for next year.

## Step 1 — Pick the category

Choose the single best-fit category Muddy offers in Essex (for example a clinic, aesthetics, hair and beauty, or wellbeing category). Enter one category only, so the vote is not split.

## Step 2 — Self-nominate the moment nominations open

Nominate Visage Aesthetics as soon as the window opens. Early nominations build momentum.

## Step 3 — Mobilise the people who already love us (this is how it is won)

Ask, warmly and clearly, in this order:

1. **In clinic.** A small card at reception and a quiet ask at the end of each appointment: "We have been nominated for a local award, it would mean a lot if you would vote, it takes ten seconds." Have the link or a QR code ready.
2. **WhatsApp and follow-ups.** A short, friendly message to recent happy patients with the direct link.
3. **Instagram and Facebook.** A simple post and stories: what the award is, why it matters, and the link. Repeat gently across the voting window, not just once. Pin it. Add it to stories highlights.
4. **Email, if there is a list.** One short note to past patients with the link.
5. **Existing Google reviewers.** These people already took the time to praise us. They are the most likely to vote.

## Step 4 — Make voting effortless

- Always share the direct link to the Visage entry, not the Muddy homepage.
- Use a QR code on the reception card so people can vote on the spot.
- Keep the ask short and human. No begging, no spam. One clear thing: please vote, here is the link.

## Step 5 — Keep it warm through the window

- A light reminder mid-window and a final one near the close. Two or three nudges, not daily.
- Thank everyone publicly at the end, win or lose. The goodwill is worth more than the result.

## The messaging (Visage voice, adapt freely)

Short social or WhatsApp version:

"We have been nominated for a Muddy Stilettos Essex award, and it would genuinely mean a lot. If we have ever looked after you well, a quick vote would make our week. It takes about ten seconds, here is the link: [direct link]. Thank you so much."

In-clinic card version:

"Thank you for trusting us. We have been nominated for a local Muddy Stilettos award. If you have a moment, a vote would mean the world to a small clinic. Scan the code to vote, it takes ten seconds."

## The rules

- Free only. Muddy is free, keep it that way. Do not buy any paid Muddy feature or package.
- One clear ask. Never spam. Be gracious throughout.
- No patient is ever pressured. A warm invitation, never an obligation.
