# Ready Award Entry — Visage Aesthetics (reusable, free entries only)

A reusable written entry for free-to-enter awards (for example Business Awards UK Essex or Health & Wellbeing, or the English Hair & Beauty Awards). It is written in Bernadette's voice. Before submitting, confirm on the official page that the award is free to enter and free to accept, then paste the relevant parts into that award's exact questions. If accepting a win would cost money, stop and check with Giles first.

Adapt the wording to each award's questions. Do not invent figures. Where a number would strengthen the entry (review count, years open, patients seen), drop in the real, current figure from the clinic's own records before submitting.

---

## Suggested category

Best small business, customer service, health and wellbeing, or new business, depending on what each award offers. For beauty and aesthetics awards, the best clinic or best aesthetics category.

## One-line summary

Visage Aesthetics is a nurse-led, non-surgical aesthetics clinic in Braintree, Essex, named Best Non-Surgical Aesthetics Clinic 2026, Essex by the Health, Beauty & Wellness Awards.

## About the business (around 150 words)

I am Bernadette Tobin, a registered nurse and Independent Nurse Prescriber with an MSc in Advanced Practice, and I founded Visage Aesthetics to do non-surgical aesthetics the medically responsible way. Aesthetics in the UK is largely unregulated. Anyone can inject, with very little training. I wanted to give local people a clinic where the person treating them is a senior clinician, where every treatment starts with a real consultation, and where the honest answer is sometimes no.

We are a small, calm clinic on Friars Lane in Braintree. We offer anti-wrinkle injections, dermal and lip filler, Profhilo, skin treatments and more, all planned around what suits the individual rather than what sells. Our whole approach is natural, subtle results and unhurried care. In 2026 that approach was recognised when we were named Best Non-Surgical Aesthetics Clinic, Essex.

## What makes us stand out (around 150 words)

Three things set us apart. First, we are genuinely nurse-led and prescriber-led. Treatments are planned and carried out by a registered nurse, not a non-medical operator, and that clinical judgement runs through everything we do. Second, we are honest to a fault. We would rather lose a sale than treat someone who does not need it, and patients tell us that is exactly why they trust us and why they come back. Third, we treat anxious first-timers with real care. A lot of people come to us nervous, having heard horror stories, and our job is to make them feel safe and informed before anything happens.

The result is a loyal local following, a strong bank of genuine reviews, and an industry award that reflects clinical standards rather than marketing.

## Customer care (around 120 words)

Every patient begins with an unhurried consultation, with no pressure and no upselling. We explain the options, the risks, the realistic outcome, and the cost, and we are happy for someone to go away and think about it. After treatment we follow up personally and are easy to reach for any aftercare questions. We listen to feedback and act on it. The reviews our patients leave, unprompted, describe the same things again and again: feeling listened to, never rushed, and getting natural results they are happy with. For us, good care is not a department, it is the whole point of the clinic.

## Why we should win (around 100 words)

Because we show that aesthetics can be done safely, honestly and to a clinical standard, and that doing it that way builds a better business. We are proof that a small, local, nurse-led clinic can hold its own against louder, less careful competitors, and be recognised for it. Winning would mean a great deal to a small team, and it would help even more local people find a clinic they can trust with their face and their health.
