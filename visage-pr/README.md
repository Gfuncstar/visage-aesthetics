# Visage Aesthetics — Press & Marketing Operation

This folder is the home of Visage Aesthetics' local press and marketing operation. It is the clinic's own version of the system used by the sister brand Aesthetics Unlocked, rebuilt from scratch for a **local clinic** rather than a national education platform. The two brands stay completely separate. Nothing here mixes Visage and Aesthetics Unlocked.

## What Visage is (and what this operation is for)

Visage Aesthetics is a small, nurse-led, non-surgical aesthetics clinic on Friars Lane in Braintree, Essex, founded by Bernadette Tobin. The goal of this operation is **local awareness that turns into clinic bookings** (consultations and treatments), from people in and around Essex. It is not about selling courses or reaching practitioners. Every piece of outreach points a local reader towards booking a consultation.

## The one command

Type **RUN PR VISAGE** and the full cycle runs: check the inbox and reply to anything waiting, send the next three to five local pitches, update the tracker, then give you a short plain-English summary. The runbook for this is [RUN-PR-VISAGE.md](RUN-PR-VISAGE.md).

## The non-negotiables

- Visage's warm, reassuring, professional clinic voice. No hype, no AI tells.
- British spelling. The full name "Visage Aesthetics". One clear ask per message.
- Patient privacy first. No patient photo or story is ever used without explicit written consent.
- You approve anything that spends money or makes a firm commitment.
- Outreach goes out one at a time, never on CC, only to verified addresses, from the clinic's own mailbox.
